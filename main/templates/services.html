Line Of Credit for Invoice Factoring
Invoice factoring is a substitute for conventional financing that raises cash next to the value of your open invoices in substitution for the right to collect against them. You can be accepted in as little as 3-5 business days with minimal paperwork, even if you are a startup or have a bad/low credit and collections history. To make it fast for you to get capital, we are offering you a Line Of Credit for Invoice Factoring.
What are the requirements and interest rates to apply for a credit line?
We will use data from 3 bureaus to get access to your credit history, and these bureaus are Vukode, Ziteso, and Sendesta. After evaluating your credit history, we will make a decision for your loan application. Interest rates will be decided according to your credit score.
